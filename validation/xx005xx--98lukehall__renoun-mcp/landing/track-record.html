Methodology

ReNoUn classifies structural regimes from OHLCV market data using 17-channel pattern analysis. It does not predict price direction -- it classifies whether conditions are bounded, active, or unstable.

  • Bounded regime: Price expected to stay within a tight envelope (typically less than 1.5%). Action: proceed at full size.
  • Active regime: Dynamic conditions with moves up to 2.5%. Action: reduce position by exposure scalar.
  • Unstable regime: Structure fragmenting. Action: avoid the trade entirely.
  • Grading: Each prediction is graded after 60 minutes against the actual price outcome. If the absolute move stayed within the regime's threshold, it passes.
  • No cherry-picking: Every single prediction is recorded and graded. The data on this page is the complete, unedited log.
  • Timestamps are UTC: All predictions are timestamped at creation time in UTC. Grading occurs automatically 60 minutes later.

Raw data: track_record.json -- download and verify independently.
