Frequently asked questions

How does metered billing work?
50 calls/day are always free — no credit card required. If you need more, add a payment method at harrisoncollab.com/billing. Calls beyond 50 are billed at $0.02 each, invoiced monthly via Stripe. Without a payment method, calls stop at 50.
What do I get for $0.02/call?
Regime type (bounded/active/unstable), action (proceed/reduce/avoid), exposure scalar (0.0-1.0), DHS score, constellation pattern, and stability half-life estimate — how long the current regime is expected to last.
What does the finance overlay do?
It takes OHLCV candle data and measures structural disorder in price action using the 17-channel engine. It outputs a regime classification and exposure scalar (0.0-1.0) that tells you how much to scale your position. 100% bounded regime accuracy across 265+ graded predictions.
Is this financial advice?
No. ReNoUn is a structural risk measurement tool, not a trading signal. It measures disorder, not direction. Use it as a pre-trade risk gate, not a signal.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Remove your payment method to revert to the free tier. No contracts, no commitments.
