Listing Description

Welcome to this charming and well-maintained 1.5-storey home located on Loch Lomond Road in East Saint Johnin a large spacious lot! Perfect for first-time homebuyers or anyone looking for a cozy home with room to grow, this property offers comfort, convenience, and excellent value. Inside, youll find a bright and inviting layout featuring two bedrooms upstairs, with the potential to easily convert back to a three-bedroom home if desired. The main floor offers the convenience of laundry access, while the kitchen and bathroom are enhanced by beautiful skylights that fill the home with natural light. This home has been lovingly cared for and includes a central heat pump system for efficient year-round comfort, as well as a full basement offering plenty of storage or future opportunities. Step outside and enjoy the large private lot, spacious deck, and detached double-car garage ideal for hobbies, vehicles, or additional storage. Located in a desirable East Saint John neighborhood, this property is close to schools, shopping, restaurants, and retail amenities, with quick and easy access to the airport. Outdoor enthusiasts will also love being just minutes from the scenic Little River Reservoir, featuring walking trails and a beach area to enjoy throughout the seasons. A warm and welcoming home in a fantastic location this could be the perfect place to start your homeownership journey (29924903)

Location Description

Stay on Highway 1 past East Saint John.Take Exit 137 for Route 111 East (signed for the Saint John Airport / Fundy-St. Martins).Follow Route 111 straight for about 5 km, then take the exit for Loch Lomond Road.Turn right onto Loch Lomond Road; the destination will be shortly down the road on your right.
