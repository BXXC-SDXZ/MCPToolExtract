## What is Meta & TikTok Ads MCP?

Agent 1st Ads MCP is a Model Context Protocol server that gives AI agents the ability to create and manage real ad campaigns on Meta (Facebook/Instagram) and TikTok — entirely through tool calls.

No logging into dashboards. No manual campaign setup. No human in the loop.

Your agent calls one tool. A complete campaign — targeting, creative, budget, and ad — is live in seconds.

---

## Why use it?

Running ads normally requires logging into Ads Manager, building campaigns manually, setting targeting, uploading creatives, and constantly checking performance. It takes hours per week and requires a human to do it.

With this MCP, your AI agent handles all of it:

- **Creates full campaigns** with a single tool call — campaign, ad set, creative, and ad are all built together
- **Controls spend in real time** — pause a campaign, increase a budget, or kill it entirely on command
- **Reads performance data** — impressions, clicks, CTR, spend, and conversions pulled directly from the ad platform
- **Runs 24/7 without you** — perfect for autonomous agents managing ad budgets automatically

---

## How it works

1. You connect the MCP server to your AI agent (Claude, Cursor, or any MCP-compatible client)
2. You provide your ad platform credentials and a license key
3. Your agent can now call tools like `create_meta_campaign`, `get_meta_campaign_stats`, and `pause_tiktok_campaign` directly

The agent never needs to visit a browser or click through a UI. Everything happens through tool calls.
